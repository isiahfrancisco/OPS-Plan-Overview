OPS Plan Overview - Fiscal Year Internal Help Desk

Prepared by: Isiah Francisco, Help Desk Supervisor

Introduction

Hello, I'm Isiah Francisco. As the Help Desk Supervisor, I am presenting our Operations Plan Overview for the upcoming fiscal year. This website outlines our mission, vision, and strategic objectives designed to enhance support efficiency, customer satisfaction, and overall service quality across the organization.

Reason for the Plan

This plan serves as a roadmap for our help desk team to align operations with organizational priorities. It ensures consistent service delivery, measurable improvement, and transparent performance tracking. By focusing on key performance metrics, the plan aims to strengthen user confidence in IT support services and enable employees to work without unnecessary technical disruptions.

Mission

To provide dependable, responsive, and customer-focused technical support that minimizes downtime and empowers staff to perform their duties efficiently.

Customer Benefit

Our users receive faster resolutions, proactive communication, and reliable service delivery, all of which contribute to improved productivity and a smoother workplace experience.

Vision

Our vision is to build a proactive, data-driven help desk recognized for excellence in service delivery and continuous improvement. Over the next three years, we aim to transition from a reactive support model to a predictive and knowledge-based operation that anticipates user needs and reduces recurring issues.

SMART Goals - Fiscal Year

Goal 1: Raise Staff Satisfaction by 12%

Measure quarterly internal staff surveys and exit interview data from October 2025 to June 2026.

User Benefit: A motivated support team delivers faster and higher-quality assistance.

Goal 2: Increase Customer Satisfaction by 10%

Use biweekly feedback surveys (October 2025 – January 2026) to track improvements.

User Benefit: Users experience better communication, empathy, and reliable service.

Goal 3: Lower Call Abandon Rate by 25%

Monitor weekly call system data to reduce missed calls between October 2025 and January 2026.

User Benefit: Faster connections and reduced wait times improve overall support accessibility.

Goal 4: Cut Average Resolution Time by 20%

Compare weekly ticketing reports (October 2025 – April 2026) against baseline to identify improvement.

User Benefit: Faster issue resolution reduces downtime and boosts productivity.

Goal 5: Improve First Contact Resolution by 8%

Analyze monthly tickets (October 2025 – April 2026) resolved on first contact and train staff accordingly.

User Benefit: More problems solved in a single interaction save time and increase satisfaction.

Fiscal Year Operations Focus

The fiscal year will emphasize:

• Continuous improvement through data analytics and feedback.

• Enhanced training for Tier 1 and Tier 2 agents.

• Stronger collaboration between Help Desk and Infrastructure teams.

• Consistent reporting and visibility into key performance indicators.

This plan is designed to maintain operational excellence while adapting to the organization’s evolving technology landscape.

Open to Feedback

Feedback and collaboration are critical to our success. Team members and leadership are encouraged to share suggestions on measurable outcomes, process improvements, or new tools that can further enhance our performance.
